Words of Welcome

Welcome to Messiah Baptist Church

I do appreciate your visit in our website.

As you navigate the pages thru our homepage, I hope you will be blessed and uplifted how God has blessed the ministry here in our place. The ministry of this church is centered in the preaching of the Word of God. It is our belief that the Bible has all the answers for those perplexing questions we face in these challenging days.

If you are looking for a church full of common people who sincerely love God, and want to serve Him and be a blessing to you and to your family. You've found it. I would count it a privilege to welcome you to our services and be our special guest. We would be honored for you to choose Messiah Baptist Church as your place of worship, so come and grow with us.

Thank you for visiting our website.

Take a few moments to look over the different pages. You may find the information interesting and helpful. If there is anything I can do to be a blessing, please do not hesitate to contact me. It is my pleasure to serve you in any way. The MBC family and I will be glad to meet you.

Hope to see you in our church real soon!

Your servant for Jesus Christ' sake,

Andres C. Guevara Jr.
Pastor
Messiah Baptist Church of Baesa
